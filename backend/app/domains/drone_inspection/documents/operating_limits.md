# Inspection Drone Operating Limits

## Mission Continuation Limits

The drone may continue a planned inspection only when battery health is at least 75 percent,
ESC temperature is below 78 C, motor vibration is below 5 mm/s, and GPS drift remains under
3 meters. Any two medium warnings should be treated as a degraded condition.

## Return To Launch Policy

For degraded conditions, the safe default is return to launch. The operator may continue
manual flight only to clear people, traffic, water, or energized assets. The system should
create a maintenance action before the next inspection mission.

## Evidence Grounding Policy

Diagnostics must cite the operating limit or maintenance section used to justify a decision.
If no source supports the recommendation, the answer must say that the decision is based on
signal thresholds only.
